Internship Opportunity

We're looking for passionate individuals to join us as interns. Dive deep into the India-Italy business relationship, work remotely, and make a significant impact.

Internship Details:

  • Location: Remote
  • Duration: 6 months
  • Commitment: 12-16 hours per week
  • Application Deadline: October 30, 2023

Responsibilities:

  • Research and compile information on women-led businesses and entrepreneurship opportunities in both India and Italy.
  • Assist in organizing virtual events, webinars, and networking sessions to promote collaboration among women entrepreneurs.
  • Support the development of content for the council's website, social media platforms, and marketing materials.
  • Liaise with stakeholders and members of the council to coordinate initiatives and partnership opportunities.
  • Help in creating mentorship and professional development programs for aspiring women leaders.
  • Prepare reports on the impact of women-led businesses in the bilateral trade between India and Italy.

Requirements:

  • Passionate about empowering women...
  • Excellent communication skills...
  • Passionate about empowering women in the business world and promoting gender equality.
  • Excellent communication skills in English (knowledge of Italian or Hindi is a plus).
  • Strong research and analytical abilities to gather and present relevant data.
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office and social media platforms.
  • Self-motivated, proactive, and able to work effectively both independently and in a team.
  • pursuing a degree in business, law, international relations, gender studies, or a related field (undergraduate or graduate).
